How SuratPro Works

From signing up to getting paid — everything is transparent, secure, and built around your success.

For Freelancers
Find great work, get paid securely
1
Create Your Profile

Build a standout profile with your skills, portfolio, work history, and hourly rate. First impressions matter.

2
Browse & Submit Proposals

Search jobs that match your expertise. Write targeted proposals with your timeline and bid to win the project.

3
Work Under a Contract

Once hired, a milestone-based contract is created. Deliver work per milestone so expectations are always clear.

4
Deliver & Get Paid

Submit milestone deliverables for client approval. Payment is released from escrow instantly upon approval. No chasing invoices.

For Clients
Hire top talent, manage with confidence
1
Post a Job

Describe your project in detail — scope, budget, timeline, and required skills. It takes less than 5 minutes.

2
Review Proposals

Receive competitive proposals from verified freelancers. Compare ratings, portfolios, and bids side by side.

3
Hire & Set Milestones

Accept a proposal and fund the escrow. Break the project into milestones so both sides stay aligned throughout.

4
Approve & Release Payment

Review each milestone delivery. Approve to release escrow funds — or request changes. You're always in control.
